🚀 Getting Started

How do I invite team members to Opsis Suite? +
Go to Company Settings → Users & Roles, then click "Invite User". Enter their email, assign a role, and select which products they can access. They'll receive an email with setup instructions.
Can I customize Opsis Suite for my industry? +
Yes! During onboarding, select your industry and we'll customize terminology, modules, and workflows specifically for your sector. You can change this later in Settings.
How do I add more products to my plan? +
Visit the Marketplace to browse available products. Click "Add Product" and we'll calculate your new pricing with bundle discounts. Changes take effect immediately.

📦 Warehouse

How do I track equipment maintenance? +
In Warehouse, select any equipment and go to the Maintenance tab. You can schedule maintenance, set hour-based alerts, track service history, and receive automatic reminders.
Can I import existing inventory from Excel? +
Yes! Go to Warehouse → Import and upload your Excel file. We support CSV and XLSX formats. Download our template to ensure your data maps correctly.

💳 Billing & Plans

How does pricing work when I add products? +
Each product has a base price, but we offer bundle discounts when you combine multiple products. The more you add, the more you save. Check the Marketplace for current pricing.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, there are no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. Your subscription remains active until the end of your billing period.

🔒 Security & Privacy

Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 standards. Your data is backed up daily and stored in secure data centers.
Can I enable two-factor authentication? +
Yes! Go to Settings → Security and enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps like Google Authenticator, Authy, and SMS-based verification.

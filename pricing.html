Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing adjusts accordingly.
What happens after the free trial?
After your 30-day trial, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or your account will be paused. No automatic charges.
Do you offer discounts for annual plans?
Yes! Annual plans include a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Toggle the switch above to see annual pricing.
What kind of support do you provide?
We offer email support for all plans, priority support for Pro plans, and dedicated 24/7 support for Enterprise customers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

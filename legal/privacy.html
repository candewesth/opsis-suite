1. Information We Collect

Opsis Suite collects information necessary to provide our warehouse management services effectively and securely.

Account Information

  • Company name, address, and contact details
  • User names, email addresses, and job titles
  • Role assignments and permission levels
  • Billing and payment information

Warehouse Data

  • Equipment inventory and specifications
  • Loan requests and approval workflows
  • Maintenance schedules and history
  • Project assignments and location tracking
  • User activity logs and system usage

Multi-Tenant Security

Each company's data is completely isolated. Your warehouse information is never visible to other Opsis Suite customers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to provide and improve our warehouse management services:

  • Service Delivery: Process equipment loans, track inventory, manage maintenance schedules
  • User Management: Authenticate users, enforce role-based permissions, provide dashboard access
  • Analytics: Generate warehouse performance reports, utilization statistics, and quality metrics
  • Communication: Send system notifications, maintenance alerts, and important updates
  • Support: Provide technical assistance and resolve service issues
  • Billing: Process payments and manage subscription services

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to warehouse management services.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Opsis Suite does not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting providers (currently Google Sheets, migrating to Firebase/PostgreSQL)
  • Payment processors for billing and subscription management
  • Email service providers for system notifications

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Opsis Suite, our users, or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice)

No Data Mining

We never analyze your warehouse data for competitive intelligence, market research, or to benefit other customers. Your operational data remains confidential to your organization.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your warehouse data:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Multi-tenant architecture with complete data isolation
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Automated backups and disaster recovery procedures

Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Principle of least privilege access
  • Regular security policy reviews and updates
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security practices to protect your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal and business data:

Access and Portability

  • View all data associated with your account through the dashboard
  • Export your warehouse data in standard formats (CSV, JSON)
  • Request a complete copy of your data

Correction and Updates

  • Update account information and user profiles
  • Correct equipment details and maintenance records
  • Modify role assignments and permissions

Deletion and Account Closure

  • Delete individual records or equipment entries
  • Close your account and request data deletion
  • Retain specific data for legal or business continuity reasons

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@opsis-suite.com or through your account administrator.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data based on business necessity and legal requirements:

Active Account Data

  • Retained while your subscription is active
  • Available for immediate access and operations
  • Continuously backed up for data protection

Closed Account Data

  • Warehouse data: 90 days after account closure
  • Billing records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Support tickets: 3 years for service improvement
  • Anonymized usage analytics: Indefinitely

You can request immediate data deletion, though we may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Opsis Suite uses cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our services:

Essential Cookies

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Dashboard preferences and settings

Analytics Cookies

  • Website usage and performance monitoring
  • Feature usage analytics for product improvement
  • Error tracking and system optimization

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of Opsis Suite.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to account administrators
  • In-dashboard announcements
  • Updated "Last Modified" date on this page

Your continued use of Opsis Suite after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

1 Information We Collect

Opsis Suite collects information necessary to provide our warehouse management services effectively and securely.

Account Information

  • Company name, address, and contact details
  • User names, email addresses, and job titles
  • Role assignments and permission levels
  • Billing and payment information

Warehouse Data

  • Equipment inventory and specifications
  • Loan requests and approval workflows
  • Maintenance schedules and history
  • Project assignments and location tracking

Multi-Tenant Security

Each company's data is completely isolated. Your warehouse information is never visible to other customers.

2 How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to provide and improve our warehouse management services:

  • Service Delivery: Process equipment loans, track inventory, manage maintenance schedules
  • User Management: Authenticate users, enforce role-based permissions
  • Analytics: Generate warehouse performance reports and utilization statistics
  • Communication: Send system notifications and important updates
  • Support: Provide technical assistance and resolve service issues
  • Billing: Process payments and manage subscription services

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to warehouse management.

3 Data Sharing

Opsis Suite does not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties, except in limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting providers (Google Sheets, Firebase/PostgreSQL)
  • Payment processors for billing and subscription management
  • Email service providers for system notifications

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect rights, property, or safety of users
  • In connection with merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

No Data Mining

We never analyze your warehouse data for competitive intelligence or to benefit other customers. Your operational data remains confidential.

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your warehouse data:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Multi-tenant architecture with complete data isolation
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Automated backups and disaster recovery

Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Principle of least privilege access
  • Regular security policy reviews and updates
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

5 Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal and business data:

Access and Portability

  • View all data through the dashboard
  • Export warehouse data in standard formats (CSV, JSON)
  • Request a complete copy of your data

Correction and Updates

  • Update account information and user profiles
  • Correct equipment details and maintenance records
  • Modify role assignments and permissions

Deletion and Account Closure

  • Delete individual records or equipment entries
  • Close account and request data deletion
  • Retain specific data for legal or business continuity

6 Data Retention

We retain your data based on business necessity and legal requirements:

Active Account Data

  • Retained while your subscription is active
  • Available for immediate access and operations
  • Continuously backed up for data protection

Closed Account Data

  • Warehouse data: 90 days after account closure
  • Billing records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Support tickets: 3 years for service improvement
  • Anonymized usage analytics: Indefinitely

Data Deletion

You can request immediate data deletion, though we may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations.

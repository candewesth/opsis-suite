Compliance Standards

Building with security and compliance in mind from day one.

Data Protection

We're building Opsis Suite with data protection regulations in mind, including GDPR for EU customers and CCPA for California residents.

Current practices: Data encryption, user consent management, data portability tools, and clear privacy policies.

Roadmap

Full GDPR and CCPA compliance frameworks will be implemented before public launch in 2026.

Security Standards

We follow industry best practices for application security, including secure coding standards, regular vulnerability testing, and encryption protocols.

Implementation: Multi-tenant data isolation, role-based access controls, secure authentication, and encrypted communications.

Future Certifications

We're evaluating SOC 2 Type II certification as we scale to enterprise customers.

Business Continuity

Disaster recovery and business continuity plans are being developed to ensure service reliability and data availability.

Current measures: Automated backups, cloud redundancy, and documented incident response procedures.

Development Stage

As a beta platform, we're continuously improving our operational resilience.

Transparency & Audits

We believe in transparency about our security and compliance practices. Our policies and procedures are documented and reviewed regularly.

Commitment: Clear communication about our practices, regular policy updates, and responsiveness to customer compliance questions.

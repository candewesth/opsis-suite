Regulatory Compliance

Opsis Suite is committed to meeting the highest standards of data protection and regulatory compliance across Mexico and the United States.

🎯 Target Compliance: January 2026
🇺🇸

United States

Federal & State Regulations

  • 🔒 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • 🛡️ California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)
  • 🏥 Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
  • 💼 Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) - For Public Companies
  • 🏛️ Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Guidelines
  • 📊 SOC 2 Type II Compliance Framework
🇲🇽

México

Federal Privacy Regulations

  • 📜 Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares (LFPDPPP)

  • 🔐 Instituto Nacional de Transparencia (INAI) Guidelines
  • 💻 Ley de Instituciones de Crédito (Banking Regulations)
  • 📋 NOM-151-SCFI-2016 Data Security Requirements
  • ⚖️ Código de Comercio (Electronic Commerce)

2025-2026 Compliance Roadmap

Our systematic approach to achieving full regulatory compliance across both Mexico and the United States by January 2026.

Q1 2025
Foundation & Assessment
Comprehensive compliance audit, gap analysis, and policy framework development for US and Mexico regulations.
Q2 2025
Technical Implementation
Enhanced data encryption, access controls, audit logging, and privacy-by-design architecture updates.
Q3 2025
Process & Documentation
Data processing agreements, consent management, incident response procedures, and staff training programs.
Q4 2025
Certification & Validation
Third-party audits, compliance certifications, penetration testing, and final validation processes.
Jan 2026
Full Compliance Launch
Official compliance certification, ongoing monitoring systems, and continuous improvement processes.
